A Collective Memoir

"This track in its entirety is based on a sound tag from the famous 1990s commercial. The one which was deeply imprinted in the subconscious of almost any kid who was watching TV at the time. It was stretched exponentially using modular patches and digital methods to form a minimalistic scratch of the half-forgotten memory of our mutual past. It is also a homage to my personal heroes Pan Sonic, who used to be called slightly different in their early days until the corporate monsters of that time made them change it." –– L
